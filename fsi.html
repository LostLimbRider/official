Family Support Initiative

Supporting the families behind every rider.
Because recovery is stronger when no one stands alone.

Our Mission

An amputation impacts far more than the individual. It affects spouses, children, parents, caregivers, friends, and everyone who walks beside them. Lost Limb Riders created the Family Support Initiative to provide education, encouragement, fellowship, and resources for the entire family throughout the recovery journey.

Who Can Participate?

  • Spouses & Partners
  • Children
  • Parents
  • Grandparents
  • Siblings
  • Caregivers
  • Immediate Family Members
  • Support Persons

Family Orientation

Every new member and family is welcomed through an orientation introducing Lost Limb Riders, our mission, volunteer opportunities, event safety, membership expectations, and available support resources.

Support Services

  • Monthly Family Support Meetings
  • Peer-to-Peer Encouragement
  • Caregiver Support Groups
  • Educational Workshops
  • Family Cookouts
  • Holiday Celebrations
  • Community Events
  • Hospital & Rehabilitation Visits
  • Resource Referrals
  • Mental Health Resource Information

Children Matter Too

Children often struggle to understand limb loss. Lost Limb Riders provides opportunities for family-friendly activities, games, educational events, and community gatherings where children can simply enjoy being kids while meeting others who understand.

Community Resources

  • Rehabilitation Services
  • Prosthetic Providers
  • Veterans Resources
  • Employment Assistance
  • Housing Information
  • Transportation Resources
  • Disability Benefits
  • Counseling Referrals

Volunteer Responsibilities

  • Treat every family with dignity.
  • Respect confidentiality.
  • Listen without judgment.
  • Avoid medical or legal advice.
  • Encourage hope and independence.
  • Represent Lost Limb Riders professionally.

Our Commitment

Recovery does not happen alone. When families receive encouragement, education, and community, everyone grows stronger. Lost Limb Riders is committed to helping every family discover hope, purpose, and a supportive community that lasts well beyond recovery.
